FAQS
WHAT SHOULD I WEAR TO ROCK CLIMB?
Wear any clothes you feel comfortable working out or moving in. Some climbers prefer to wear pants to protect their legs from scrapes + scuffs.
WHAT IS BOULDERING?
Bouldering is a style of climbing done on shorter walls-it requires no ropes and no partner. If you have any further questions about bouldering, feel free to ask one of our friendly staff!
WHAT GEAR DO I NEED?
Climbing shoes (rental shoes are included with your day pass) are designed with a snug fit and outsoles of soft + sticky rubber to help you grip footholds on the wall. Chalk (available for rental) is optional: it helps keep your hands dry to improve your grip.
