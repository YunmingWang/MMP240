About Go Paws

Go Paws is an online dog walker company in NYC.

It helps people to find a loving dog walker to walk their dogs.

What is Dog Walker?

A dog walker is a profession that involves a person walking with a dog, usually from the dog's home and then returning the dog back to its home. Walking a dog is part of the daily exercise regime needed in order to keep a dog healthy and happy.

Where we service:

We service all of Manhatten, Queens, and Brooklyn!
