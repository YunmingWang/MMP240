Events
BIRTHDAYS AND PRIVATE PARTIES

Urabn Bouldering is a really fun place to host your birthday party - for kids and adults alike! Bouldering (climbing without ropes) is a super engaging activity, and we're certain you and your friends will want to come back for more!

TEAM BUILDING EVENTS

Teams that work well together have awesome interpersonal relationships. Our corporate bouldering events provide an exciting way for teammates to learn about each other. Climbing is a powerful way to explore team dynamics while having tons of fun and getting a great workout in!
