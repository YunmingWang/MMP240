Urban Bouldering

About

Urban Bouldering is an indoor climbing gym in New York City. It started in 1992, since then we've been striving to build a community where everyone feels safe and comfortable to grow physically and mentally stronger! In our climbing gym, whether new or experienced we hope to introduce climbers to seeing climbing in a different view. We want our climbers to have fun and inspire one another.

UB climbing gym is more than a workout opportunity. It is a community, a place to discover your inner strength and share the fun with other like-minded adventure seekers.
